How to use together: A high Step 1 mortality score does not contraindicate SSRF — it contextualizes the risk. Use Step 1 output to anchor the goals-of-care discussion, then Step 2 to determine operative indication. High risk + SSRF indicated = operate with documented informed consent. Critical risk + relative contraindications = individualized multidisciplinary decision.
